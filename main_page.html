Welcome!

This is the Vermont Data Exploration App, a protype for a website where you can access, analyze, share, and request open source data about Vermont. We're designing the App to meet your needs, so please look around and remember to give us feedback!

Usage

Use the sidebar on the left to navigate between the different sections of this app. Each link in the sidebar will take you to a separate page with its own content and tools.

Caveats

The Vermont Data Exploration App is still in a private prototype phase. Please don't share this link, and remember that both the design and content are subject to change.
